    My Story 💁🏻

    I have a background in fine art and have always loved creating art in many mediums from an early age. After spending some time exploring other fields of work, I realized that being a part of an industry where I can unleash my creativity and help others is the most fulfilling.

    I enjoy bringing a vision to life- not only through design but by connecting the emotional and functional components that are necessary to create something worthwhile. Whether it be through business, personal or social endeavors, making a lasting positive impact on the human experience is the ultimate win.

    I'm currently based out of Niagara Falls, Canada.
